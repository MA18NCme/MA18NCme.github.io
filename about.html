About Me

Melany Alvarez
A girl who had always dreamed of becoming an artist ever since she was young and when the opportunity was given she took it.
She knows how to us Photoshop, Illustrator and InDesign.
And also knows how to create logos, menu designs, magazine spreads, packaging, web designs, etc.
Putting her all into any project is important to her, always striving to put 100% into anything she does.
Expressing her creativity and being able to create things is something that fills her life with joy.
But both in and out of work she is still the same creative, curious, expressive, and adventurous girl she has always been.
Besides Graphic Designing she loves to draw, create illustrations, write, and hopes to learn how to animate.
And You will never catch her without her sketchbook.
She brings it everywhere be it while she is at school, at work, or traveling.
It is a way where she can create her own worlds and express herself freely.
But besides enjoying her hobbies and interests she absolutely loves spending time with her family and close friends.
She has a great relationship with her parents and younger sister, usually doing almost everything together and being incredibly close to each other.
Melany also enjoys listening to music: mostly stuff from musicals
While she does like music from famous artists like Imagine Dragons or Shawn Mendes, she absolutely adores musicals like Dear Evan Hansen, Be More Chill, In The Heights, and Hamilton.
